How to Use the ODL Website

Step 1: Contact your administrater

To get started, create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the home page. Fill in your details and verify your email address.

Step 2: Log In

Use your email and password to log in to the system. Ensure your credentials are secure and not shared with others.

Step 3: Access Courses

Navigate to the "Courses" section to view available learning materials, lectures, and assignments.

Step 4: Submit Assignments

Upload your assignments by selecting the course and choosing the relevant assignment submission link.

Step 5: Contact Support

If you have any issues, visit the "Support" section to raise a ticket or check the FAQ for quick solutions.
